India Tourism

Parthasarathy Temple
Parthasarathy Temple is a historic Hindu temple in Chennai, dedicated to Lord Krishna.
Taj mahal
The Taj Mahal is a 17th-century marble mausoleum in Agra, India.
Mysore Palace
Mysore Palace, in Karnataka, is a grand royal residence known for its Indo-Saracenic architecture.
Kerala
Kerala, India, known for its backwaters, beaches, and lush greenery.
Jodhpur
Jodhpur Junction is a major railway station in Rajasthan, known for its connectivity.
Kailashnath Temple
Kailashnath Temple in Ellora, Maharashtra, is a monolithic rock-cut temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.
The Gateway of India
The Gateway of India, located in Mumbai, is a historic arch monument built in 1924 to commemorate King George V's visit.
Buddha Park of Ravangla
Buddha Park of Ravangla in Sikkim features a stunning 130-foot statue of Buddha, amidst serene landscaped gardens.
Modhera Sun Temple
The Modhera Sun Temple in Gujarat, India, is a 11th-century temple dedicated to the Sun God, Surya.
